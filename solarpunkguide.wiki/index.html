What Is Solarpunk?

Solarpunk is an upcoming open-world survival crafting game set on floating islands in a bright, hopeful future. Build your home, harness solar energy, construct airships, and explore the skies — all while living in harmony with nature.

It's a game about creativity, sustainability, and adventure. No dark dystopias here — just you, the sun, and an endless sky of possibilities.
