Inside Cyberwave: How a Two-Person Team Built Solarpunk

Solarpunk’s development story is a big part of why so many players are excited for it. Behind the game is Cyberwave, a small team with a very clear creative vision.

A Small Team with a Big Idea

Cyberwave’s approach shows that a small team can still create something memorable when the idea is strong and the execution is focused.

Why the Project Stood Out

Players responded quickly to the game’s peaceful tone, floating island setting, and fresh take on survival crafting.

What Makes the Team Interesting

The most interesting part of Cyberwave’s story is not just the size of the team, but the clarity of the game’s identity. They knew exactly what kind of experience they wanted to make.
