Solarpunk Tech Tree Guide: 15 Key Early Unlocks

Solarpunk’s tech tree is one of the most important parts of progression. This guide helps you choose the early unlocks that matter most.

What to Unlock First

In general, you should prioritize survival, energy, and basic infrastructure before moving into convenience and automation.

Top Early Priorities

  • 🛠️ Basic tools
  • 🏗️ Core building pieces
  • 💧 Water collection
  • ⚡ Solar power and batteries
  • 🌱 Farming basics
  • 🌬️ Wind power
  • 🚀 Airship-related progression

Why Order Matters

A good unlock order helps you stay stable and prevents you from spending research on things you cannot use yet.

Advertisement

Recommended Approach

Start with essential survival tools, then move toward energy and farming, and finally into automation and transport.

Final Takeaway

The tech tree is easiest to manage when you think about your current needs first. Unlock what will help your next step, not just what looks interesting.
