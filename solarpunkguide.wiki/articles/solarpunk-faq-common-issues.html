Solarpunk FAQ: Common Issues, Fixes & Launch Day Troubleshooting Guide

June 8, 20267 min read

Launch day is here — and with millions of players jumping into Solarpunk simultaneously, questions are inevitable. This FAQ covers every common issue across installation, gameplay, multiplayer, and technical troubleshooting. Bookmark this page.

💿 Installation & Launch Issues

Q: When does the game unlock?
Global unlock: June 8, 2026 at 10:00 AM UTC. PDT: 3 AM · EDT: 6 AM · BST: 11 AM · CEST: 12 PM.
Q: When can I pre-load?
Pre-load began June 6, 2026 at 10:00 AM UTC for all pre-order players.
Q: How much storage space?
~20 GB. Reserve at least 25 GB. SSD strongly recommended — mechanical drives will cause long load times and stuttering.
Q: Which operating systems are supported?
Windows 10/11 64-bit only. No 32-bit, no Windows 7/8.x.
Q: Game won't launch — what do I do?
① Verify game file integrity (Steam: right-click → Properties → Local Files → Verify). ② Update GPU drivers. ③ Run as Administrator. ④ Temporarily disable antivirus/firewall. ⑤ Install latest DirectX and Visual C++ runtimes.

🎮 Gameplay Questions

Q: Is there a death penalty?
Almost none. Falling off an island costs 15% hunger/thirst. You respawn at your bed. No item loss, no skill loss, no permadeath.
Q: Is there combat?
Zero. No monsters, no enemies, no weapons. This is a purely peaceful survival builder.
Q: How do I drink water?
⚠️ Do not drink lake or seawater — it dehydrates you. Build Rainwater Collectors or a Well, then use a Bucket to collect drinkable water.
Q: How do I build an airship?
Research Airship Technology → collect 10 Airship Components (scattered across your starter island) → gather 50 Wood + 30 Iron Ingots + 1 Solar Panel → build at an Airship Dock.
Q: Is there a time limit?
None. Play indefinitely at your own pace. No countdowns, no forced endings.

👥 Multiplayer Questions

Q: How many players in co-op?
Up to 4 players online.
Q: Is cross-platform multiplayer supported?
No. Same-platform only. PC with PC, Xbox with Xbox, etc.
Q: Whose computer saves the game?
The host. If the host disconnects, the session pauses.
Q: Does each player have their own inventory?
Yes. Independent backpacks — no loot conflicts.
Q: Are researched technologies shared?
Yes. One player unlocks a tech → the entire team can use its recipes.

🔧 Technical Issues

Q: Game is stuttering — how do I fix it?
Lower resolution and graphics preset · Close background apps · Update GPU drivers · Move game to SSD · Enable DLSS or FSR if supported.
Q: Game keeps crashing — what should I try?
Verify game files · Update GPU drivers · Lower shadow quality and anti-aliasing · Disable overclocking · Try Windowed mode.
Q: Does the game support controllers?
Yes. Xbox and PlayStation controllers are fully supported.
Q: Is Steam Workshop supported?
Yes. Share and download mods, building blueprints, and custom skins.

📋 Other Common Questions

Q: Physical edition?
Digital only at launch. No physical release announced.
Q: Will there be DLC?
Free updates for at least 1 year come first. Paid DLC may follow — no concrete plans yet.
Q: Mac or Linux support?
Not planned. The two-person team is focused entirely on Windows and consoles.
Q: Can I still get pre-order bonuses?
No. Pre-order bonuses were permanently retired at 10:00 AM UTC on June 8, 2026. They will not return.
