Solarpunk Airships Guide: Build, Fly, Upgrade & Explore by Airship

The airship is Solarpunk's signature feature — the moment you first lift off from your island and see the clouds parting beneath you is the moment the game truly opens up. This guide covers everything: building your first airship, mastering flight, upgrading for speed and cargo, and running an efficient multiplayer flight crew.

🔓 Unlocking & Building Your First Airship

Requirements

  • 🔬 Research Airship Technology (Tier 3 tech, requires Tier 2 Research Table + Iron Ingots + Cloth).
  • 🏗️ Build an Airship Dock on flat ground at your island's edge: Concrete×10 + Iron Ingot×5 + Solar Panel×1.

Flight Pro Tips

  • 📏 Low altitude (below 50m): Slower, less energy drain. Best for short hops and precision landings.
  • ☁️ High altitude (100m+): Faster, higher energy drain. Best for long-distance travel between distant islands.
  • 🪂 Landing: Align with a flat area, tap Shift to descend gently, tap S to brake before touchdown. Never land on slopes or cliff edges.
  • ☀️ Clear weather = infinite range. Cloudy = range halved. Rain/thunderstorm = do not fly — wind throws you off course and visibility is near zero.

🚫 Airship Mistakes to Avoid

  • ⛈️ Flying in storms — wind will throw you off course. Just wait it out.
  • 📦 Overloading — exceeding cargo capacity reduces speed, increases energy drain, and can prevent takeoff entirely.
  • 🏔️ Flying blind at high altitude — floating debris and small uncharted islands can shred your envelope. Stay alert.
  • 🔧 Skipping maintenance — airship parts degrade with use. Regularly dock and repair, or risk a mid-flight breakdown.
  • 🔋 Long-range flights without a return plan — cloudy weather can halve your range mid-trip. Always keep enough battery for the return journey.
