Solarpunk Launch Day Checklist: Complete Step-by-Step from Spawn to Airship

June 8, 20266 min read
In This Guide
  1. Loading contents…

Solarpunk launches globally on June 8, 2026 at 10:00 AM UTC. This minute-by-minute checklist will take you from a bare-handed castaway to an airship captain in exactly 3 hours. Follow each phase in order — every step builds on the last.

🎯 Goal for Hour 3: A weatherproof house · 10+ auto-irrigated farmland plots · Full solar + wind energy grid · Iron workbench + furnace · Storage system · A flyable airship · One explored neighboring island.

⏱️ 0–10 Minutes: Secure Survival Basics

  • 🍓 Gather 20 berries from spawn-area bushes (restores hunger + thirst)
  • 🪵 Collect 10 branches + 10 stones from the ground
  • 🪓 Craft Stone Axe + Stone Pickaxe + Build Hammer (press F)
  • 🌲 Chop 3 trees → ~30 wood
  • ⛏️ Mine 5 mossy green rocks → ~10 stone
  • 🔨 Build Workbench on flat central ground near water

⏱️ 10–30 Minutes: Establish Starter Base

  • 🔬 Build Research Table next to Workbench
  • 💧 Research Rainwater Collection → build 2 Collectors + 1 Bucket
  • 🌾 Research Basic Farming → till 5 plots, plant 3 berry + 2 cotton
  • 🏠 Build a small wooden house + Bed (skips night, restores stamina)
  • 🔥 Build a Campfire for cooking and nighttime light

⏱️ 30–60 Minutes: Build Your Energy Grid

  • ⚡ Research Basic Energy → build 2 Solar Panels (place on high ground) + 2 Batteries
  • 🔌 Wire: Solar Panels → Batteries → Workbench + Research Table
  • 🔥 Research Smelting → build 1 Furnace
  • ⛏️ Find iron ore veins (red-speckled rocks) → mine 20 ore → smelt into Iron Ingots

⏱️ 60–90 Minutes: Upgrade & Automate

  • ⚙️ Upgrade to Iron Workbench
  • 🪓 Craft Iron Axe + Iron Pickaxe (60% faster gathering)
  • 💧 Research Auto-Irrigation → build 3 Irrigators (cover all farmland)
  • 💨 Research Wind Power → build 1 Wind Turbine on highest point
  • 📦 Build 2 Storage Chests — sort by category

⏱️ 90–120 Minutes: Expand & Fortify

  • 🐔 Research Basic Animal Care → build Chicken Coop + 2 Feeding Troughs
  • 🌾 Gather 10 wheat seeds → plant 5 wheat plots
  • 🪏 Craft Iron Shovel (faster tilling)
  • 💧 Build 1 Well (stable water source, weather-independent)
  • ♻️ Build 1 Recycler (convert scraps to points)

⏱️ 120–150 Minutes: Collect Airship Components

  • 🔍 Explore every corner of the starter island
  • 🧩 Collect 10 Airship Components — check: island edges · small caves · near abandoned structures
  • 🌲 Gather extra wood and iron ore for airship construction
  • 🛩️ Research Airship Technology
  • 🏗️ Build Airship Dock on flat ground at island's edge

⏱️ 150–180 Minutes: Build & Fly Your Airship

  • 📦 Airship materials: 10 Components + 50 Wood + 30 Iron Ingots + 1 Solar Panel
  • 🛩️ Build your first Solar Airship at the dock
  • 🕹️ Test flight: WASD to move, Space to ascend, Shift to descend
  • ☁️ Circle your island, then fly to the nearest neighboring island
  • 🌱 Land, gather new resources and seeds, mark the location on your map

📋 Optional Launch-Day Extras

  • 💾 Back up your save (minor bugs still possible)
  • ⚙️ Tweak graphics settings for best performance
  • 💬 Join the official Discord to coordinate with other players
  • 📱 Follow official socials for patch notes and updates

🚫 Critical Beginner Mistakes

  • 🌲 Clear-cutting forests — leave saplings. They regrow.
  • 🧶 Wasting cotton — it's for research and cloth, not clothes.
  • ☀️ Solar-only energy — always add a wind turbine by hour 2.
  • 🏗️ Random building placement — plan zones: energy (high), farm (flat), living (scenic).
  • 💧 Drinking lake/seawater — it dehydrates you. Use Rainwater Collectors or Wells only.
