Solarpunk Beginner Roadmap: Perfect 3-Hour Start on Launch Day

May 30, 20268 min read
In This Guide
  1. Loading contents…

Dropping into Solarpunk for the first time can feel overwhelming — floating islands everywhere, a vast tech tree, and no obvious "correct" path. This roadmap gives you a minute-by-minute plan for your first 3 hours, taking you from bare-handed castaway to airship captain with a fully functional self-sufficient base.

⏱️ First 10 Minutes: Secure Survival Basics

🍓 Immediately gather 20+ berries — they restore both hunger and thirst. Don't touch trees yet.
🪵 Pick up 10 branches + 10 stones from the ground around your spawn point.
🪓 Craft a Stone Axe + Stone Pickaxe (press F for quick-build menu).
🌲 Chop 3 trees (≈30 wood). Mine 5 mossy stones (≈10 stone).
🔨 Craft a Build Hammer + Workbench. Place the Workbench on flat ground near water.

⏱️ First 30 Minutes: Establish Your Starter Base

🔬 Build a Research Table next to your Workbench — this unlocks everything else.
💧 Research + build 2 Rainwater Collectors + a Bucket. Your water problem is now solved.
🌾 Till 5–10 farmland plots with a Stone Shovel. Plant berry and cotton seeds.
🏠 Build a small wooden shelter (3×3 or 4×4) with walls, a door, and a roof. Place a Bed inside.
🔥 Build a Campfire outside — cook food for better nutrition and use it for light at night.

⏱️ First 1 Hour: Build Your Energy Grid

☀️ Research + build 2 Solar Panels on high ground (higher = more efficient).
🔋 Research + build 2 Batteries to store daytime power for nighttime use.
🔌 Wire everything together: Solar Panels → Batteries → Workbench + Research Table.
🔥 Research + build a Furnace. Mine 20 iron ore (red-speckled rocks), smelt into Iron Ingots.

⚠️ Don't skip batteries! Without them, your entire base shuts down at night. Batteries are not optional — they're the heart of your energy system.

⏱️ First 2 Hours: Automate & Upgrade

⚙️ Upgrade to an Iron Workbench using iron ingots.
💧 Research + build Auto-Irrigators — cover every 3×3 farmland grid. Hands-free watering.
💨 Research + build 1 Wind Turbine on the highest point. Supplements solar at night and in storms.
⛏️ Research + build 2 Auto-Miners on stone/iron deposits. Passive resource income.
📦 Build 4–6 Storage Chests. Sort by category: raw materials, crafted goods, food, tools.

⏱️ First 3 Hours: Build Your Airship & Explore

🔍 Collect 10 Airship Components — scattered across the starter island (check edges, caves, high ledges).
🛩️ Research Airship Technology, then build an Airship Dock at the island's edge.
📦 Craft your first Solar Airship: 10 components + 50 wood + 30 iron ingots + 2 solar panels.
🕹️ Test flight: WASD to move, Space to ascend, Shift to descend. Practice landing on a nearby island.
🗺️ Explore your first neighboring island — gather new seeds, rare resources, and mark the location on your map.

🚫 Common Beginner Mistakes to Avoid

  • 🏗️ Overbuilding too early — More buildings = more power consumption. Expand gradually.
  • ☀️ Relying only on solar — Always add a wind turbine for nighttime and cloudy weather.
  • 🌿 Wasting cotton — Use early cotton for research and cloth, not decorative clothing.
  • 🌲 Clear-cutting forests — Leave saplings. They regrow. Sustainability is the whole point.
  • 💾 Not backing up saves — The game is polished but still new. Back up daily.

🎯 After 3 Hours You Should Have: A weatherproof house with a bed · 10+ farmland plots with auto-irrigation · 2 solar panels + 1 wind turbine + 2 batteries · Iron Workbench + Furnace · 2 Auto-Miners running · A flyable airship · One explored neighboring island. From here, the entire sky is yours. ☀️
