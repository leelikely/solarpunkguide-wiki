Solarpunk Co-op Guide: Best 4-Player Roles & Teamwork Strategies

May 30, 20265 min read
In This Guide
  1. Loading contents…

Solarpunk supports up to 4 players in online co-op, and while you can all just do whatever you want, a little coordination goes a long way. Here's the optimal role breakdown that transforms a chaotic group into a sky-home-building machine.

🏗️ Role 1: The Architect (Builder)

Core Mission: Design and build the base — everything from layout planning to final decoration.

Daily Tasks: Plan functional zones (energy, farming, production, living) · Build all structures · Decorate and beautify · Maintain roads, bridges, and stairs.

Recommended Tech Priority: Construction → Furniture → Advanced Building Materials.

Playstyle Fit: Creative thinkers who love designing beautiful spaces. If you spent hours building in Minecraft or The Sims, this is your role.

⚡ Role 2: The Engineer

Core Mission: Build and maintain the energy grid, automation systems, and all technical infrastructure.

Daily Tasks: Design and expand the power grid (solar + wind + batteries) · Build and optimize auto-miners, drones, and irrigators · Troubleshoot power outages · Plan wireless power coverage.

Recommended Tech Priority: Energy → Automation → Wireless Power → Drone Logistics.

Playstyle Fit: Systems thinkers who love optimizing efficiency. Satisfactory and Factorio players will feel right at home.

🌾 Role 3: The Farmer

Core Mission: Keep the team fed and supplied with agricultural materials.

Daily Tasks: Till and plant farmland · Manage irrigation · Breed and care for animals · Harvest and process crops · Trade surplus with merchants for rare items.

Recommended Tech Priority: Farming → Animal Care → Advanced Crops → Greenhouse.

Playstyle Fit: Patient, nurturing players who find joy in watching things grow. Stardew Valley veterans, this is you.

🛩️ Role 4: The Explorer

Core Mission: Pilot the airship to discover new islands, gather rare resources, and bring back blueprints.

Daily Tasks: Build and maintain airships · Scout and mark islands on the map · Collect rare ores, seeds, and treasure chest items · Find and repair merchant platforms · Bring back new tech and resources for the team.

Recommended Tech Priority: Airship → Exploration → Airship Upgrades.

Playstyle Fit: Adventurous, curious players who always want to see what's over the next cloud. If you loved the exploration in Zelda: Breath of the Wild, this role is for you.

🤝 Teamwork Best Practices

  • 📢 Use voice chat (Discord, Steam voice) — text is too slow for real-time coordination.
  • 📦 Agree on a shared storage system — label chests, use consistent categories, and don't hoard resources others need.
  • 🔬 Coordinate tech unlocks — only one person needs to research a technology for the whole team to benefit. Avoid duplicate research.
  • ⏰ Hold a quick 2-minute planning session at the start of each session. "What's our goal today?" prevents four people doing four unrelated things.
  • 🎨 Respect playstyles — the Farmer might not care about min-maxing solar panel angles, and the Engineer might not care about flower arrangements. That's the point of roles.
