Solarpunk Tech Tree Guide: All Technologies, Research Costs & Unlock Times (2026)

June 3, 20267 min read
In This Guide
  1. Loading contents…

The Solarpunk tech tree is the backbone of your entire progression. Every tool upgrade, every automation device, every building type, and even your ability to fly between islands — it all flows through research. This guide catalogs every technology across all three tiers, with exact research material costs, timings, prerequisites, and what each unlock gives you. Use it to plan your ideal research order and never waste precious resources on a low-priority tech again.

⏱️ Research Time Reference: 1 real-world minute = 30 in-game minutes. All research durations below are listed in in-game hours. For example, a 60-minute research time = 2 real-world minutes. All research is conducted at the Research Table — upgrade it to access higher tiers.

🌱 Tier 1: Foundation Tech (0–90 Game Hours) — Research Table Lv.1

Tier 1 covers everything you need to survive and establish your first base. These are your starter technologies — all accessible from a Level 1 Research Table with no prior research required for the first node.

🎯 Optimal Tier 1 Order: Basic Tool Crafting → Basic Construction → Rainwater Collection → Basic Farming → Solar Energy Basics. This sequence ensures you always have shelter before you need it and water before you start farming. Don't rush Solar Energy — you need glass, which requires a Furnace, which requires smelting tech (Tier 2).

⚙️ Tier 2: Industrialization (90–360 Game Hours) — Research Table Lv.2

Tier 2 is where your base transitions from survival camp to industrial hub. You'll unlock metalworking, automation basics, and advanced farming. All Tier 1 technologies must be completed before any Tier 2 research becomes available.

⚠️ The Tier 2 Trap: Many players rush Iron Tool Forging first because "better tools = faster everything." This is a mistake. Research Auto-Irrigation before Iron Tools. Here's why: auto-watering saves you ~15 minutes of manual labor per in-game day, which you can then spend mining the extra iron you'd need anyway. Automation first, upgrades second.

🚀 Tier 3: Skyward Expansion (360+ Game Hours) — Research Table Max Level

Tier 3 is the endgame tech tree — airships, drones, wireless power, and full automation. These technologies fundamentally change how you play. Requires all Tier 2 technologies completed and a fully upgraded Research Table.

🎯 Optimal Tier 3 Order: Automated Mining → Drone Logistics → Airship Engineering → Wireless Power → Waste Cycling. Rush Airship Engineering as fast as possible — inter-island travel is the single biggest progression unlock in the entire game. It gives you access to Crystal Islands (rare crystals for drones and wireless power) and Desert Islands (sandstone for mass glass production).

📋 Section 4: Bonus Rules & Hidden Mechanics

👥 Co-op Research Bonus

In a 4-player co-op session, all research times are reduced by 30%. This stacks multiplicatively with the Research Table-Workbench synergy bonus (~15%). Combined, a 4-player team researching with optimal placement can complete the entire tech tree in roughly 60% of the solo time. Any one player completing a research project instantly unlocks it for the entire team.

🔮 Hidden Side-Tech: Trade Knowledge

After fully repairing a Merchant Platform and completing at least 5 trades, a hidden technology node called "Trade Knowledge" appears at your Research Table. Researching it (cost: 200 Trade Coins + Crystal Shard ×2, time: 60 min) allows you to purchase high-tier blueprints directly from merchants instead of researching them — a massive shortcut for late-game tech. This is never mentioned in any in-game tooltip.

🎮 Demo Version Limitations

The free Steam demo includes all Tier 1 and partial Tier 2 technologies only. Tier 3 and the full Tier 2 branch (Advanced Farming, Energy Storage, Large Wind Turbines) are exclusive to the full release. Demo save files do transfer to the full version, so your research progress is preserved.
