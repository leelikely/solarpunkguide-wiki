Solarpunk Trading Guide: All Merchant Prices, Refresh Timers & Platform Repair (2026)

June 3, 20266 min read
In This Guide
  1. Loading contents…

Trading is one of Solarpunk's most overlooked but incredibly profitable systems. Once you repair a Merchant Platform, you gain access to rare seeds, advanced components, blueprints, and a steady income stream. This guide covers everything — platform locations, repair costs, full price tables, refresh mechanics, and pro trading strategies to maximize your trade coin earnings.

💰 What Are Trade Coins? Trade Coins are the game's universal merchant currency. You earn them by selling resources to merchants and spend them on rare items you can't craft. They're not physical inventory items — they appear as a counter in the merchant UI. In co-op mode, all 4 players share the same coin balance and merchant inventory.

🏪 Section 1: Merchant Platform — Locations & Repair

Merchant Platforms are ruined structures found on specific island types. You cannot build them from scratch — you can only find and repair them.

📍 Where to Find Merchant Platforms

Large Forest Island 🌳100% (guaranteed one per island)Look for a broken stone altar with faded purple trading-post banners near the island center
Desert Capital Island 🏜️100% (guaranteed one per island)Found near the central ruins — larger and more ornate than forest variants
Ruin Island 🏛️100% (guaranteed one per island)Ancient stone platform with glowing runes — requires the most materials to repair

🔧 Repair Cost & Requirements

🧱 Material📦 Quantity🌿 How to Obtain
Stone Bricks 🧱20Craft at Basic Workbench: Stone ×2 → Bricks ×1
Iron Ingot 🔩10Smelt in Furnace: Iron Ore ×2 + Log fuel → Ingot ×1
Glass 🔍5Smelt in Furnace: Sandstone ×2 → Glass ×1 (mine sandstone on Desert Islands)

🔬 Tech Prerequisite: You must research Basic Trade at a Level 2 Research Table before you can repair a Merchant Platform. Without this tech, the "Repair" interaction won't appear even if you have all the materials.

👥 Co-op Note: Each large island has only one Merchant Platform. In a 4-player session, everyone shares the same trading interface and inventory. The coin balance is pooled — spend wisely and communicate with your team before making large purchases!

💰 Best Money-Making Strategy: Build 2 Pig Stys as soon as you unlock Advanced Ranching. Pair with 2 Sheep Pens (wool at 12 coins/2 days) for a combined ~25 coins/day of fully passive income.

🔄 Section 4: Inventory Refresh & Price Fluctuation Rules

The merchant economy is dynamic — prices shift based on your trading behavior, and inventory refreshes on a predictable timer. Understanding these mechanics is the difference between a profitable trader and a broke one.

📅 Refresh Schedule (All times UTC)

🔄 Refresh Type⏰ Timing📦 What It Affects
Daily Standard RefreshUTC 00:00 (midnight)All common stock — seeds, cloth, glass, basic components
3-Day Rare RefreshEvery 3 in-game daysRare items — blueprints, Crystal Shards, Airship Component Kits
Rainy Season SpecialEvery Wednesday (UTC)Exclusive: Rainwater Collector blueprints and Greenhouse building materials — only available during this window!

📈 Supply & Demand Price Fluctuation

The merchant uses a dynamic pricing algorithm based on your trading volume:

  • 📉 Selling too much? If you sell more than 50 units of a single resource in one day, the merchant's buy price for that item drops 12% the next day.
  • 📈 Buying too much? If you buy out a specific item's entire stock, its sell price increases 10% the next restock.
  • 🎯 Price Range: Fluctuations cap at ±25% from base price. Prices never go above 125% or below 75% of the listed values.
  • ⏳ Reset: Prices return to baseline after 7 in-game days of no trading activity for that specific item.

⚠️ Avoid the Dumping Trap: Selling 100 logs at once crashes the log buy price for a full week. Instead, sell diverse resource batches — 30 logs + 30 wheat + 20 wool keeps all prices stable while maximizing your coin income. Diversify or lose profit.

⚡ Manual Quick-Refresh Trick

Want to force a merchant inventory refresh without waiting for the daily reset? Here's the trick:

  1. All players leave the island where the Merchant Platform is located.
  2. Travel to a neighboring island (any adjacent floating island works).
  3. Wait 24 in-game hours on that island (roughly 48 real-world minutes).
  4. Return to the Merchant Platform — all inventory is refreshed.

This works in both single-player and co-op and is the fastest way to cycle for rare blueprints.

💡 Section 5: Pro Trading Strategies

🥇 Strategy #1 — Truffle Empire: Truffles sell for 38 coins each — the highest raw resource price in the game. Build 3 Pig Stys (9 pigs total) for a passive income of ~114 coins every 3 days. That buys you 2–3 Airship Component Kits per week with zero active effort.

🥈 Strategy #2 — Buy Low, Sell High Seasons: Rare seeds are cheapest during non-rainy days (45 coins). Buy them in bulk during dry periods, grow the crops, then sell the成品 (finished products) — not the raw seeds — for significantly more value. One Glow Sunflower harvest can net 120+ coins from a 45-coin seed investment.

🥉 Strategy #3 — Wednesday Rainy Day Priority: Every Wednesday (UTC), merchants stock exclusive items — Rainwater Collector blueprints and Greenhouse materials — that are unavailable any other day. Set a reminder. If you miss Wednesday, you wait a full week. These items are essential for advanced base-building and cannot be obtained any other way.
