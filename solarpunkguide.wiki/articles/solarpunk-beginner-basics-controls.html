🖥️ UI Overview

  • Top-left: Health (red), Hunger (orange), Thirst (blue) — three bars. Reaching zero only applies minor penalties; you don't die.
  • Top-right: Minimap showing explored islands, resource nodes, and your buildings.
  • Bottom bar: Quick-access hotbar for tools, food, and building materials.
  • Right side: Mission hints and objective tracker — follow these for guided progression.

⏱️ Your First 30 Minutes — Survival Priority

🍓 Gather 20 berries from bushes around spawn (restores hunger + thirst).
🪵 Collect 10 branches + 10 stones from the ground.
🪓 Craft Stone Axe + Stone Pickaxe (press F → Tools tab).
🌲 Chop 3 trees (≈30 wood). Mine 5 mossy green rocks (≈10 stone).
🔨 Build a Workbench + Research Table. Place on flat ground near water.
💧 Research Rainwater Collector → build 2. Craft a Bucket.
🌾 Till 5 farmland plots. Plant berries and cotton seeds.
🏠 Build a small wooden house + Bed. The bed lets you skip night and recover stamina.
☀️ Build 1 Solar Panel + 1 Battery. Wire them to your Workbench. No more darkness.

🚫 5 Beginner Mistakes to Avoid

  • 🌲 Clear-cutting trees — leave saplings. They regrow.
  • 🌿 Wasting cotton — early cotton goes to research and cloth, not decorations.
  • ☀️ Relying only on solar — add a Wind Turbine by hour 2 for nighttime and cloudy days.
  • 🏗️ Building randomly — plan zones: energy (high ground), farming (flat center), living (scenic edge).
  • 🔬 Ignoring the Research Table — it unlocks everything. Upgrade it as soon as possible.

📋 First-Hour Summary

By the 60-minute mark you should have: a weatherproof shelter with a bed · 5+ farmland plots · 2 Rainwater Collectors · a Research Table · 1 Solar Panel + 1 Battery · a Campfire · a Storage Chest with organized supplies. From here, everything expands — automate your farm, upgrade to iron tools, and start collecting airship components. ☀️
