🎮 All Supported Platforms

Solarpunk launches simultaneously across all announced platforms. Every version includes the exact same content — no platform-exclusive features or content to worry about.

💻 PC Platforms

  • Steam — Full Workshop support, cloud saves, achievements
  • Epic Games Store — Cross-platform cloud saves (PC only)
  • GOG — DRM-free version for offline play enthusiasts

🎮 Console Platforms

  • PlayStation 5 — DualSense haptic feedback & adaptive trigger support
  • Xbox Series X|S — Xbox Play Anywhere (buy once, play on Xbox & PC); Day-one Game Pass
  • Nintendo Switch 2 — One of the first launch-window titles for Nintendo's next-gen console; handheld & docked modes

⚠️ Important Solarpunk does not support last-gen consoles (PS4, Xbox One, original Switch). The console ports are handled by Mi'pu'mi Games (known for work on Hitman and Control). There are currently no plans for Mac, Linux, or mobile versions.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will there be a physical edition?

At this time, only digital editions have been announced. No physical release information is available.

Q: Can I play offline?

✅ Yes. Single-player mode is fully playable offline. Online connectivity is only required for multiplayer.

Q: Do saves transfer between platforms?

🔄 Partial. Xbox Play Anywhere enables save transfers between Xbox and Windows PC. Cross-save between other platforms is not currently supported.

Q: Is cross-platform multiplayer supported?

❌ No. Multiplayer is limited to same-platform play. PC players can play with other PC players, Xbox with Xbox, etc.

Q: Will there be DLC?

The developers have stated their focus is on free content updates for at least the first year. Paid DLC may come in the future but nothing is planned yet.

Q: What's the age rating?

👶 ESRB: Everyone · PEGI: 3+ — suitable for all ages. No violence, no scary content, no mature themes.
