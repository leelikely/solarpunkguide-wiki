Solarpunk Pre-Order Guide: Standard vs Deluxe Edition — Which One Is Right for You?

May 30, 2026 7 min read
In This Guide

We're less than 10 days from Solarpunk's global launch on June 8, 2026, and pre-orders are now live across all platforms. But with Standard and Deluxe editions on offer — plus a stack of pre-order bonuses — which option gives you the best bang for your buck? This guide breaks down every detail so you can decide with confidence.

⏰ Time-Sensitive Pre-order bonuses are only available until June 8, 2026 at 10:00 AM UTC. After launch, these items will be permanently unavailable. If you want the bonuses, don't wait.

All platforms are currently offering a 10% launch discount on pre-orders — this discount expires at launch. Here's the full breakdown:

📦 Standard Edition — What You Get

The Standard Edition includes the complete game with absolutely no content missing:

  • ✅ Full Solarpunk game
  • ✅ Single-player mode
  • ✅ 4-player online co-op
  • ✅ All floating islands, all technology trees, all building items
  • ✅ At least 1 year of free content updates (developer commitment)

If you just want to play the game — build, farm, explore, and fly airships — the Standard Edition is everything you need.

🌟 Deluxe Edition — What You Get

The Deluxe Edition includes everything from Standard, plus these exclusive digital extras:

BonusDetails
🎵 Digital Soundtrack30+ original tracks in MP3 and lossless FLAC formats
🎨 Digital Art Book100+ pages of concept art, character designs, island sketches, and architectural drawings — never-before-seen content
🖼️ 4K Wallpaper Pack10 exclusive wallpapers for desktop, tablet, and phone
💬 Discord RoleExclusive gold badge + access to a developer-only Discord channel
🎁 All Pre-Order BonusesEverything listed in the pre-order section below

💰 Value Check The soundtrack and art book alone would typically sell for $15+. All Deluxe extras are purely cosmetic and collectible — they provide zero gameplay advantage. Buy Deluxe because you love the art and music, not because you need an edge.

🎁 Pre-Order Bonuses (Both Editions)

Pre-order either edition before June 8, 2026 at 10:00 AM UTC and you'll receive these permanent, never-again-available items:

🪴 "Spring Garden" Furniture Set (12 pieces)

A beautifully themed outdoor collection including: garden bench, garden table, planter stand, hanging flower baskets, decorative fountain, stone pathway tiles, picket fence gate, solar garden lamp, butterfly feeder, decorative birdhouse, sunflower ornament, and rose bush. Every piece features unique spring-inspired designs more detailed than the standard in-game furniture.

🌻 Golden Sunflower Seeds

A rare seed variety with 50% higher yield and 30% faster growth than regular sunflowers. Harvested golden sunflowers can be used to craft premium dyes and unique decorations. Only one set per save file — cannot be obtained through any other in-game method.

🌈 "Rainbow Bridge" — Multiplayer Cloud Island Decor

Build a stunning rainbow bridge connecting two of your floating islands. Available in multiplayer mode only, this structure changes colors dynamically with the in-game time and weather. A badge of honor that tells other players you were here from day one.

🛩️ Early Airship Unlock

Normally, unlocking airship construction takes approximately 3 hours of gameplay. Pre-order players can build airships from the very start — no unlock requirements, no waiting. Take to the skies the moment you spawn in.

🔴 Final Call These four bonuses will be permanently removed from sale the moment the game launches. There are no plans to re-release them. If you care about exclusive cosmetics, pre-order now.

🔄 Refund Policies

  • Steam — Standard policy: refund within 14 days of purchase with less than 2 hours of playtime
  • Epic Games Store — Similar policy: 14 days / 2 hours
  • GOG — 30-day refund window, even after playing
  • PlayStation / Xbox / Nintendo — Standard platform refund policies apply

🏆 Final Recommendation

You Should Buy…If You…
🎯 Standard EditionJust want to play the game. You don't care about soundtracks, art books, or exclusive cosmetics. You want the full game at the best price.
⭐ Deluxe EditionLove the art style and music. Want to support a two-person indie team. Enjoy collecting digital extras. The extra $10 gives you items worth significantly more.
🎮 Xbox Game PassAlready have Game Pass and just want to try the game. You can always buy the Deluxe upgrade later if you fall in love with it.
🔥 Pre-order either wayWant those pre-order bonuses — especially the early airship unlock and golden sunflower seeds. These genuinely improve the early-game experience.

💡 Our Pick For most players, the Standard Edition pre-order on Steam is the sweet spot — full game, all pre-order bonuses, Workshop support, and the lowest price. If you're a collector or really want to support indie developers, the Deluxe is absolutely worth the extra $10.
