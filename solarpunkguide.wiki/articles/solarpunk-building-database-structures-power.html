Planning your floating island base? This complete building database catalogs every functional structure in Solarpunk across five categories: 🏭 Production Workstations, 💧 Water & Farming, ⚡ Energy & Power, 🗺️ Exploration & Storage, and ♻️ Recycling & Ecology. Each entry includes construction costs, tech prerequisites, power consumption, and exactly what the building does — so you can plan your base layout before you place the first foundation.

📐 Base Planning Rule of Thumb: Place energy buildings on the highest elevation, production workstations in the middle layer, and farming/storage on flat ground. This vertical zoning naturally separates incompatible functions and minimizes wiring headaches.

⚡ The Golden Energy Ratio: For a starter base, build 4 Solar Panels + 2 Wind Turbines + 4 Batteries. This combination covers all basic devices (Workbench, Research Table, 4 Auto-Sprinklers) through any weather, day or night. Calculate your total daily draw before adding new powered buildings.

📊 Bonus: Power Grid Planning Formula

Before you place any powered building, use this quick checklist to avoid blackouts:

  1. 📋 List all powered buildings and their daily power draw (e.g., 4 Auto-Sprinklers = 2.0/day).
  2. ☀️ Calculate minimum daily generation — assume cloudy weather (halve solar output for safety).
  3. 🔋 Add battery capacity equal to at least 1.5× your total daily draw to survive nights and storms.
  4. ✅ Build generation FIRST, batteries SECOND, devices THIRD. Never connect a device before its power source is ready.

🧮 Quick Math Example: 4 Auto-Sprinklers (2.0/day) + Advanced Fabricator (1.5/day) + Airship Dock (0.5/day) = 4.0/day total draw. You'll need: 3 Solar Panels (5.4 clear / 2.7 cloudy) + 2 Wind Turbines (1.8/day) + 4 Batteries (20.0 storage). This gives you a comfortable buffer for any weather.
