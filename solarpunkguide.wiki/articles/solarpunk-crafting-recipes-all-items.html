🔑 Crafting Pro Tips

  • 🔬 Unlock key techs first: Basic Energy → Smelting → Auto-Farming → Airship — in that order.
  • ♻️ Use the Recycler: Convert unwanted items into recycle points, exchange for rare materials.
  • 📦 Batch-craft: The Iron Workbench supports batch crafting — produce planks, cloth, and ingots in bulk.
  • 👥 Multiplayer sharing: When one player unlocks a tech, the whole team can use its recipes. Coordinate to avoid duplicate research.
