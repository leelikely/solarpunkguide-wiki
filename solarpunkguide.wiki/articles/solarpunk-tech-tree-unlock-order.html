Solarpunk Tech Tree Guide: Best Unlock Order — 15 Must-Have Technologies

May 30, 20267 min read
In This Guide
  1. Loading contents…

The Solarpunk tech tree is vast — and spending research points on the wrong technologies early can slow your progression to a crawl. This guide recommends the 15 most impactful technologies in the optimal unlock order, organized across three phases.

🔑 Golden Rule: Survival first → Energy second → Automation third. Follow this order and you'll never be stuck without power, food, or water.

📗 Phase 1: Survival Foundation (0–30 min)

#TechnologyUnlocksPriority
1🔧 Basic ToolsStone Axe, Pickaxe, Shovel, Build Hammer★★★★★
2🏗️ Basic ConstructionWorkbench, Storage Chest, Wooden Walls/Doors/Windows★★★★★
3💧 Rainwater CollectionRainwater Collector, Bucket★★★★★
4🌾 Basic FarmingFarmland, Seed Bags, Watering Can★★★★★
5⚡ Basic EnergySolar Panel, Battery, Wires★★★★★

📙 Phase 2: Base Development (30 min–1.5 hr)

#TechnologyUnlocksPriority
6🔬 Research Table UpgradeTier 2 Research Table (unlocks all higher tech)★★★★★
7🔥 Smelting & MetallurgyFurnace, Iron Ingots★★★★★
8💨 Wind PowerWind Turbine★★★★☆
9💧 Auto-IrrigationAuto-Irrigator (covers 3×3 farmland)★★★★☆
10🐔 Basic Animal CareChicken Coop, Feeding Trough, Water Trough★★★☆☆

📕 Phase 3: Automation Begins (1.5–3 hr)

#TechnologyUnlocksPriority
11⛏️ Iron ToolsIron Axe, Pickaxe, Shovel (2× gathering speed)★★★★☆
12🤖 Auto-MiningAuto-Miner (passive stone/ore collection)★★★★☆
13🚁 Transport DronesTransport Drone, Drone Station (auto item transfer)★★★★☆
14🛩️ Airship TechnologyAirship Dock, Solar Airship★★★★★
15📡 Wireless PowerWireless Power Tower (no more cables!)★★★★☆

🔹 Optional Techs (Pick Based on Playstyle)

  • 🪑 Furniture Crafting — For builders and decorators who want 200+ customization options
  • 🌿 Advanced Farming — Unlocks rare crops like coffee, cocoa, and glowing plants
  • 🐄 Advanced Animal Care — Unlocks sheep, pigs, and cows with symbiotic mechanics
  • 💧 Hydro Power — Water generators for bases built near waterfalls

🎯 Pro Tip: Don't unlock everything just because you can. Each technology increases your base's power draw. Unlock what you need, when you need it — and always ensure your energy grid can support new additions.
