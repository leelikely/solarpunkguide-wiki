Solarpunk System Requirements: Can Your PC Run It?

May 30, 2026 5 min read
In This Guide
  1. Loading contents…

With Solarpunk's launch just days away, the number one question on every player's mind is: "Can my PC handle it?" The official system requirements have been published on both the Epic Games Store and Steam. Below, we break down exactly what you need — from bare-minimum 30 FPS gaming all the way up to a buttery-smooth 4K experience.

🖥️ Quick Verdict Solarpunk is surprisingly accessible. If you've bought a gaming PC in the last 6–8 years, you can probably run it. The minimum specs target decade-old hardware, while the recommended specs sit comfortably in mid-range territory.

🔽 Official Minimum Requirements (1080p Low, 30 FPS)

This is the floor. If your PC meets or exceeds these specs, you can play Solarpunk — but expect to dial settings down to Low and hover around 30 FPS at 1080p.

ComponentMinimum Requirement
🖥️ OSWindows 10 64-bit
🧠 CPUIntel Core i5-2500K @ 3.3 GHz or AMD FX 6300 @ 3.5 GHz
💾 RAM8 GB
🎮 GPUNVIDIA GeForce GTX 780 (3 GB) or AMD Radeon R9 290 (4 GB)
⚙️ DirectXDirectX 11
💿 Storage20 GB SSD

⚠️ Minimum = Playable, Not Pleasant At these specs, you'll want to close all background apps, disable shadows, and lower anti-aliasing to squeeze out every frame. It'll run — but the experience will be significantly compromised.

✅ Official Recommended Requirements (1080p High, 60 FPS)

This is the sweet spot. Meeting the recommended specs means smooth 60 FPS gameplay at 1080p with High settings — exactly how the developers intended most players to experience the game.

📌 Critical Notes

  • 💿 SSD is non-negotiable — The official specs say "SSD" for a reason. Running Solarpunk from a traditional hard drive will cause long load times and potential stuttering as the game streams in Unreal Engine 5 assets.
  • ⬆️ DLSS & FSR support — Solarpunk supports both NVIDIA DLSS and AMD FSR. If your GPU supports either technology, enable it. You'll get a substantial FPS boost with minimal visual quality loss.
  • 🧠 RAM matters — 8 GB is the bare minimum. If you only have 8 GB, close your browser, Discord, and any other background apps before launching the game. 16 GB is strongly recommended.
  • 🖥️ OS compatibility — Solarpunk does not support 32-bit operating systems or Windows 7/8.x. You need a 64-bit version of Windows 10 or 11.

🔧 Performance Optimization Tips

  1. 🔄 Update your GPU drivers — NVIDIA and AMD typically release game-ready drivers ahead of major launches. Check for updates on June 7 or 8.
  2. 🎛️ Tweak shadows first — Shadow quality is usually the single biggest performance hog. Dropping shadows from Ultra to Medium can gain you 15–20% FPS with barely noticeable visual difference.
  3. 📴 Close background apps — Browsers (especially Chrome), video players, and antivirus scans eat RAM and CPU cycles. Close everything non-essential before launching.
  4. 🖥️ Use Fullscreen mode — Exclusive fullscreen generally delivers better performance than borderless windowed mode. Switch in the graphics settings menu.
  5. ⚡ Enable DLSS/FSR — If your GPU supports it, this is the single most impactful setting you can change. Set it to "Quality" mode for a great balance of visuals and performance.

🎯 Bottom Line If you've bought a mid-range gaming PC or laptop in the last 5 years, you're golden. Solarpunk targets broad accessibility — the art style is beautiful without being punishingly demanding. Just make sure you have an SSD and 16 GB of RAM, and you'll have a great time.
