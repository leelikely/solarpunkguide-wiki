Solarpunk Exploration & Biomes Guide: All Island Types, Resources & Hidden Secrets

May 30, 20268 min read
In This Guide
  1. Loading contents…

Solarpunk's world is a vast sky of dozens of floating islands, each belonging to one of five distinct biomes. Every biome has exclusive resources, unique visual character, hidden structures, and treasure that can't be found anywhere else. Exploration isn't optional — it's how you progress.

🛩️ Exploration Basics

  • 🛩️ Airship required — Unlock via Airship Technology (Tier 3). Build at an Airship Dock on your island's edge.
  • 🕹️ Controls: WASD to move, Space to ascend, Shift to descend. Solar-powered — infinite range in clear weather, halved in clouds/rain.
  • 📦 Loadout: Bring tools (Iron Pickaxe minimum), empty storage chests, food/water for the journey, and spare fuel/batteries.
  • 🗺️ Mark everything: Use the minimap to tag resource nodes, treasure chests, and merchant platforms for easy return trips.

🌲 1. Forest Islands (Starter & Most Common)

AspectDetails
🌍 AppearanceDense green trees, grassy meadows, streams, abundant sunlight
💎 ResourcesWood (most abundant), berries, cotton, wheat, stone, clay, basic iron ore
🎁 Hidden LootAbandoned Research Tables, rare seed chests (coffee, cocoa), blueprint fragments
🎯 Best ForNew player base location, bulk wood farming, cotton gathering

🏜️ 2. Desert Islands (Hot & Dry)

AspectDetails
🌍 AppearanceGolden sand dunes, sparse cacti, intense sunlight, high temperatures
💎 ResourcesSand (most abundant), sandstone, sunflower seeds, rare gold ore, cactus fruit
🎁 Hidden LootGlass blueprint chests, abandoned furnaces, solar panel component caches
⚠️ HazardHigh temperature increases thirst drain — bring extra water. Avoid midday travel.

⛰️ 3. Mountain Islands (Steep & Windy)

AspectDetails
🌍 AppearanceTowering rock formations, cliffs, sparse vegetation, strong winds
💎 ResourcesIron ore (most abundant), stone, rare silver ore, coal, wind turbine parts
🎁 Hidden LootAdvanced tool blueprints, auto-miner components, floating cave entrances (rare ores inside)
⚠️ HazardSteep terrain — easy to fall. Wind makes airship landings tricky. Bring a parachute.

🔮 4. Crystal Islands (Rare & Magical)

AspectDetails
🌍 AppearanceGlowing crystal veins, luminescent plants, pale blue sky, floating crystal shards
💎 ResourcesCrystal (rare building material), glowing seeds, advanced cloth, solar panel core components
🎁 Hidden LootWireless power blueprints, airship upgrade parts, rare decoration recipes
✨ SpecialGlows at night — the most photogenic biome. Crystal shards are fragile and can break if you walk on them.

🏛️ 5. Ruin Islands (Endgame & Mysterious)

AspectDetails
🌍 AppearanceAncient crumbling stone structures, moss-covered walls, dim lighting, mysterious runes
💎 ResourcesAncient parts, rare blueprints, gold, diamond, premium decoration materials
🎁 Hidden LootRepairable merchant platforms (trade rare items), ultimate treasure chests (all rare materials + endgame blueprints)
⚠️ HazardUnstable architecture — floors can collapse. Solve rune puzzles to access the deepest vaults.

🗺️ Optimal Exploration Routes

StageRouteGoal
🌱 Early (Days 1–2)Forest → Desert → MountainSand (glass), iron ore, establish material supply
🏡 Mid (Days 3–7)Mountain → Crystal → Forest (rare seeds)Crystal, glowing seeds, airship upgrade parts
🏙️ Late (Week 1+)Large Ruin → all biomes → hidden islandsGold, diamond, ultimate blueprints, multiple bases

🔍 Explorer's Essential Tips

  • ⬆️ Upgrade your airship first — Speed, battery capacity, and cargo space make long-range exploration safer and more profitable.
  • ☀️ Watch the weather — Clear days = long-range expeditions. Stormy days = stick to nearby islands (wind is strong, visibility is low).
  • 🗺️ Mark everything — A marked map is worth 10 hours of aimless flying.
  • 👥 Co-op exploration is faster — Pilot + 2 gatherers + 1 loot organizer = 4× efficiency.
  • 🪂 Falling isn't fatal — You lose 15% hunger/thirst and respawn at your bed. Don't fear the void.
