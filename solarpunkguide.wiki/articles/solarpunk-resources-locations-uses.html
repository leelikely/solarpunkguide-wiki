Solarpunk Resources Guide: All Material Locations, Uses & Farming Tips

May 30, 20267 min read
In This Guide

Every structure, tool, and technology in Solarpunk starts with raw materials. Knowing where to find each resource — and how to farm it sustainably — is the difference between thriving and constantly scrambling. This guide catalogs every resource in the game.

🪵 Natural Resources (Mining & Gathering)

ResourceHow to GetBest LocationsPrimary Uses
🌲 WoodAxe on treesAll forest islands (abundant)Basic tools, construction, fuel
🪨 StonePickaxe on mossy green rocksAll islandsBasic tools, construction, furnace
🏖️ SandPickaxe on brown sandstoneStarter island, desert islandsGlass, concrete
🧱 ClayPickaxe on gray rocksStarter island, near riversBricks, pottery
⛏️ Iron OrePickaxe on red-speckled rocksStarter island, mountain islandsSmelt into Iron Ingots → advanced tools & structures

🌲 Sustainability Tip: Leave saplings standing — they regrow into full trees. Clear-cutting your island means you'll have to travel farther and farther for wood.

💎 Rare Resources

ResourceLocationRarityUses
🥇 GoldRuin islands, deep mountain cavesRarePremium tools, decorations, high-speed engine upgrades

🔍 Explorer's Note: Every floating island has at least one hidden chest. Check cliff edges, small caves, and behind waterfalls. The best loot — including blueprints you can't get any other way — is always off the beaten path.
