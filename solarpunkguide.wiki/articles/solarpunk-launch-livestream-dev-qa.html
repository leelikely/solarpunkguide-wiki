💬 Developer Q&A Highlights

Q: Why no Early Access?
We believe the game is complete and polished enough to stand on its own. We didn't want players to experience an unfinished version.
Q: What's coming in free updates?
At least 1 year of free content: new floating islands, more buildings and furniture, new crops and animals, expanded automation, and multiplayer improvements.
Q: Paid DLC?
Possibly in the future — but free updates come first. We'll listen to player feedback before deciding.
Q: Mac / Linux / Mobile?
No current plans. As a two-person team, we're focused entirely on PC and consoles.
Q: Steam Workshop?
Yes! Steam Workshop is supported. Share and download mods, building blueprints, and custom skins.
Q: Story mode?
Not at launch — we want players to have total creative freedom. Small narrative quests may come in future updates.
