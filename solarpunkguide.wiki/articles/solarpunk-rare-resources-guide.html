Solarpunk Rare Resources Guide: How to Get Truffles, Gold, Diamonds & More

June 23, 20269 min read
In This Guide
  1. Loading contents…

Wood, stone, and iron ore are easy — your auto-miners handle those while you sleep. But truffles, crystals, gold, and diamonds? Those require exploration, specific unlock conditions, and smart farming strategies. This guide catalogs every rare resource in Solarpunk: where to find it, how to unlock it, the most efficient farming routes, and what to spend it on (and what not to waste it on).

📊 Rare Resource Tiers

TierResourcesWhen You Need ThemDifficulty
🟢 PracticalTruffles, Crystals, Advanced ClothMid-gameMedium
🟡 PreciousGold, Ancient Parts, Airship Upgrade KitsLate-game coreHigh
🔴 UltimateDiamonds, Exclusive Blueprint ScrollsEndgame / completionistVery High

⚠️ Key fact: None of these resources can be mass-farmed on your starter island. You must build an airship and explore. No airship = no rare resources. Period.

🐷 Truffles — The "Soft Gold" You Farm at Home

DetailInfo
⭐ RarityPractical
🔓 UnlockResearch Table Tier 6 → Pig Pen (requires 5 Carrots + 5 Wood). Carrots require a Level 3 airship to reach northeastern/southern islands.
📦 MethodPigs dig truffles from soil automatically — ~1–2 per pig per day

🔥 Max Output Strategy:

  • 🌱 Build pig pens on grassy soil with tree roots — 50% higher truffle rate than concrete floors
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 1 male : 3 female ratio — fastest breeding, largest herd, highest total output
  • ⏰ Truffles persist in the ground — collect every 2–3 days, no need to check daily

Uses: Premium cooking recipes · Sell to merchants for high trade coins · Airship Level 4 upgrade (critical!)

🐷 Don't over-expand pigs early. Each pig eats daily. Only scale up once your wheat and berry supply is rock-solid, or you'll starve your own livestock.

🔮 Crystals — The Glowing Island's Bounty

DetailInfo
⭐ RarityPractical
📍 LocationMedium & large Crystal floating islands — surface veins and cave deposits
⛏️ ToolIron Pickaxe minimum (stone won't work)

🔥 Max Output Strategy:

  • 🕳️ Prioritize cave veins — denser clusters, weather-proof, no fall risk from floating shards
  • 🔄 Crystal veins respawn slightly each in-game day — don't strip-mine, do rotation runs
  • 🎒 Bring extra food and water — caves are deep and trips take time

Uses: Wireless Power Tower · Advanced Solar Panels · Airship upgrade components · Glowing decorations

🥇 Gold — Deep Earth & Ancient Ruins

DetailInfo
⭐ RarityPrecious
📍 LocationDeep mine shafts of large Mountain islands · Ruin island treasure chests

🔥 Max Output Strategy:

  • ⛰️ On Mountain islands, find vertical shafts going deep — gold spawns in the lowest layers, barely any near the surface
  • 🏛️ In Ruins, prioritize sealed chamber chests — significantly higher gold drop rate than exposed surface chests
  • 🔥 Gold ore must be smelted into Gold Ingots at a Furnace before use

Uses: Premium tools · High-end decorations · Trade for rare blueprints at merchant platforms

🥇 Gold is scarce. Do not waste it. Never spend gold ingots on common decorations. Prioritize: core equipment upgrades > merchant blueprint trades > everything else.

💎 Diamonds — The Endgame Prize

DetailInfo
⭐ RarityUltimate
📍 LocationUltimate chamber chests in large Ruin islands · ~2/3 of Winter islands have diamond nodes (spawn alongside Bell Peppers)
🔓 RequirementFully upgraded airship (all levels) to reach distant Winter islands

🔥 Max Output Strategy:

  • 🏛️ Ruin ultimate chambers require activating runes in Sun → Wind → Water order — get the sequence wrong and the door stays locked
  • ❄️ Winter island diamonds hide under shaded ice rocks — scan carefully, they blend in
  • ⚠️ Each large Ruin chest is one-time only. Once looted, that chamber is done. Find new Ruin islands for more.

Uses: Max-tier diamond tools · Ultimate decorations · Hidden tech tree branches

💎 Diamonds are irreplaceable. Store them immediately in a locked inner chest. Do not use for anything until you've unlocked the full tech tree and know exactly what you need them for.

🛩️ Airship Upgrade Parts — Exploration's Core Reward

Rarity: Precious | Sources: Crystal & Ruin island hidden chests · Merchant robot (1 unit every 3 in-game days) · Starter island gives 10 basic components only

🔥 Strategy: Check every merchant on cooldown — buy their single upgrade part every 3 days. Over weeks, this adds up to more parts than you'll find exploring. Combine with chest loot for steady upgrade progress.

🗺️ Optimal Solo Farming Route (Single Day)

🏠 Starter Island → ⛰️ Mountain Island (gold + iron ore) → 🔮 Crystal Island (crystals) → 🏪 Merchant Island (sell truffles, buy upgrade parts) → 🏠 Return

⏱️ ~2 in-game days round trip. Half a backpack of rare resources per run. Highest value-to-time ratio in the game.

👥 4-Player Co-op Route

👨‍✈️ 1 Pilot (airship steering only) · ⛏️ 2 Miners (split to different resource nodes) · 🎒 1 Looter (chests + merchant trading). 2.5× faster than solo.
