Solarpunk Early Game Roadmap: Your First 3 Hours on Launch Day

Your first few hours in Solarpunk matter a lot. This roadmap gives you a practical step-by-step plan for the opening stage so you can avoid confusion and build momentum quickly.

First 10 Minutes: Survival Basics

Start by gathering berries, sticks, and stone. Make basic tools, secure food and water, and find a good place to begin your base.

First 30 Minutes: Base Foundation

Build your first workbench, research table, and simple shelter. Start getting your early farming setup ready as soon as possible.

First Hour: Power and Progression

Move into solar power, batteries, and early furnace use. As soon as your power starts working, the rest of the game opens up much more smoothly.

Second Hour: Automation Starter Goals

Focus on auto irrigation, storage, and basic mining tools. These steps make the game feel less manual and more organized.

Third Hour: Get Ready for the Airship

Once your basics are stable, start collecting the parts needed for your first airship so you can begin exploring beyond the starting island.

Final Advice

The best opening is the one that keeps you calm and organized. Don’t rush everything at once — build a stable foundation and expand step by step.
