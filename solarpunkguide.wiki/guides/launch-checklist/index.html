Solarpunk Launch Checklist: 7 Things to Do Before Release

Launch day can feel overwhelming, but a simple checklist makes it much easier to start strong. Use this guide to get ready for Solarpunk before release.

1. Check Your System Requirements

Make sure your PC meets the minimum or recommended requirements before launch. If your specs are close to the minimum, plan to lower settings early.

2. Clear Enough Storage Space

Leave enough room for the game, patches, and save files. A clean SSD setup will help the game run and load more smoothly.

3. Pre-Load if You Can

If pre-loading is available on your platform, download the game ahead of time so you are ready the moment it unlocks.

4. Decide Which Version to Buy

If you are still choosing between editions, compare the standard and deluxe editions carefully before release.

5. Practice the Basics in the Demo

If you have time, use the demo to learn the controls and the basic game loop. Even one short practice session can make launch day much easier.

6. Prepare Your Account and Platform

Make sure your storefront account is signed in and ready to go so you do not waste time at launch.

7. Get Your Setup Ready

Check your mouse, keyboard, headset, and display settings before release so you can start playing comfortably.

Final Advice

A little preparation goes a long way. If you handle these seven things before launch, you will be in a much better position to enjoy the game right away.
