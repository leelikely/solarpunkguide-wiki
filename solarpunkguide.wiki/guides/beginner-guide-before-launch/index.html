Solarpunk Beginner Guide: Everything New Players Should Know Before Launch

With Solarpunk launching on June 8, 2026, now is the best time to prepare. This beginner guide covers the basics, key systems, and the most useful things to practice before day one.

1. Know Your PC Specs Before You Start

Before launch, make sure your computer meets the official system requirements. If your hardware is close to the minimum, lower your expectations and plan to use performance settings like DLSS or FSR if available.

2. Decide Which Edition You Want

If you are considering a pre-order, compare the standard and deluxe editions carefully. The standard edition gives you the full game, while the deluxe edition adds digital extras and bonus rewards.

3. Practice the Controls in the Demo if You Can

If a demo is available, spend a little time learning the core controls first. That includes movement, interaction, building, inventory management, and the basics of resource gathering.

4. Focus on the First Day Instead of Trying to Do Everything

When the game launches, keep your first session simple. Learn the main loop, find basic resources, and build a small stable foundation before you start expanding.

5. Learn the Core Systems Early

The most important systems to understand are energy, building, automation, farming, and exploration. Once you know how they connect, the rest of the game becomes much easier to approach.

6. Keep Your Long-Term Goals Simple

Early on, do not try to build the perfect base right away. Start with a workable plan, then improve it step by step as you learn more about the game.

Key Things New Players Should Remember

  • Check your PC specs before launch.
  • Decide in advance whether the standard or deluxe edition is better for you.
  • Use the demo or early practice time to learn the controls.
  • Keep the first day simple and focused.
  • Learn the core systems before you try to optimize everything.
