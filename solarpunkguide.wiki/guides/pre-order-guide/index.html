Solarpunk Pre-Order Guide: Standard vs Deluxe Edition and Bonus Rewards

If you are thinking about pre-ordering Solarpunk, this guide breaks down the differences between the standard and deluxe editions, the bonus rewards, and the most important things to know before you buy.

Release Timing and Platforms

Solarpunk launches globally on June 8, 2026, with PC and console versions available on release day. Pre-load timing and edition details may vary slightly by platform, so it is worth checking your storefront before launch.

Advertisement

Standard Edition

The standard edition includes the full game. If you only want the core experience, this edition is enough to enjoy everything Solarpunk has to offer.

  • Full base game
  • Access to all core gameplay systems
  • Support for solo and co-op play

Deluxe Edition

The deluxe edition includes the full game plus extra digital content such as the soundtrack, art book, wallpaper pack, and other bonus items. It is aimed more at players who want a collector-style package.

  • Full base game
  • Digital soundtrack
  • Digital art book
  • Bonus wallpaper pack
  • Extra digital rewards

Pre-Order Bonuses

Anyone who pre-orders before launch may receive exclusive bonus items. These rewards are designed to add a little extra value without changing the core game experience.

  • 🌸 Seasonal furniture set
  • 🌻 Rare golden sunflower seed
  • 🌈 Co-op themed sky decoration
  • 🛩️ Early airship unlock access

Which Edition Should You Choose?

If you mainly want to play the game, the standard edition is a strong choice. If you enjoy digital extras and want a more complete collector package, the deluxe edition may be worth the upgrade.

Final Advice

Decide based on how much you value bonus content versus simple access to the full game. If you already know Solarpunk is a game you want to spend time with, pre-order rewards may be a nice bonus.
