Solarpunk Game Overview: Build, Explore & Thrive in a Floating Island World

Solarpunk is a cozy open-world survival crafting game set in a vibrant floating island world. Build sustainable bases, harness renewable energy, farm in harmony with nature, automate workflows, and explore the skies by airship — solo or with up to four players in co-op.

What Is Solarpunk?

Solarpunk is a cozy open-world survival crafting game developed by the German indie studio Cyberwave, a two-person team that spent four years bringing the project to life. The game is published by rokaplay and Metaroot, and it is set to launch globally on June 8, 2026.

It will be available on PC via Steam, Epic Games Store, and GOG, along with PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and Nintendo Switch 2. Instead of leaning into the tension and pressure of traditional survival games, Solarpunk embraces optimism, sustainability, and a calm sense of discovery.

The World: Vibrant Floating Islands in a Hopeful Future

The game takes place in a technologically advanced floating island world where nature and human life coexist in harmony. Colorful clouds drift around islands covered in forests, flower fields, terraces, and hidden ruins. Some islands host traders and camps, while others are home to quiet exploration and discovery.

There is no wasteland, no desperate scramble for scraps, and no apocalyptic struggle for survival. Instead, the game celebrates sunlight, wind, water, and the freedom to create something meaningful.

Core Gameplay: Build, Farm, Automate & Explore at Your Own Pace

1. Freeform Base Building: Craft Your Sky Sanctuary

🌿 Solarpunk gives you a freeform building system without grid restrictions. You can use materials such as wood, stone, glass, and metal to create cozy homes, layered estates, or scenic lookout towers.

✨ The game also features a rich decoration system with more than 200 furniture and decor pieces, plus full color customization. Building upward is encouraged too — higher locations provide excellent views and can improve the efficiency of solar panels and wind turbines.

2. Renewable Energy System: Power Your World Sustainably

⚡ Energy is one of Solarpunk’s most important systems, and it fits the game’s eco-friendly theme perfectly:

  • Solar Panels: reliable during the day, with performance affected by weather.
  • Wind Turbines: generate power all day, with stronger output at higher elevations.
  • Water Generators: work best near waterfalls and stable water sources.
  • Batteries and Wireless Power: store extra energy and reduce the need for constant wiring.

3. Cozy Farming & Symbiotic Animal Care

🌱 Farming is relaxed and rewarding. You can grow a variety of crops, including berries, wheat, cotton, sunflowers, and tomatoes. Rain automatically waters your fields, so farming never feels like a chore.

🐔 Animals also fit into the game’s “living with nature” philosophy. Chickens, sheep, pigs, and cows all contribute in friendly, non-violent ways. With the right setup, you can automate irrigation and spend less time on repetitive tasks.

4. Automation & Crafting: Simplify Daily Life

🛠️ Crafting starts with basic tools and gradually opens up more advanced equipment and recipes. From stone tools to higher-tier workbenches, the game keeps progression steady and approachable.

🤖 Automation systems help reduce repetitive work. Mining machines, transport drones, and fishing devices can all simplify daily routines, while the research table unlocks more advanced crafting options over time.

5. Airship Exploration: Sail the Skies & Discover Secrets

🚀 A major highlight of Solarpunk is the airship system. Once you collect the necessary components, you can build your own solar-powered airship and explore the skies at your own pace.

Floating islands come in different types, including resource islands, merchant islands, and ruin islands. Each one offers something useful, whether it is rare materials, special blueprints, or hidden treasures.

6. Multiplayer Co-op: Build Together with Up to 4 Players

👥 Solarpunk supports up to four players in co-op. Each player has their own inventory and can take on different roles, making teamwork feel natural and flexible.

Whether you want to build, farm, explore, or manage resources, co-op lets friends work together in a relaxed and rewarding way.

What Makes Solarpunk Stand Out?

  • 🌞 No combat, pure relaxation — there are no monsters or death penalties that punish you for playing slowly.
  • 🌿 Optimistic eco-friendly theme — the game focuses on sustainability, harmony, and bright visual design.
  • ⚙️ Deep automation and creativity — you can optimize workflows without losing the cozy atmosphere.
  • ⭐ Highly anticipated indie release — it has already attracted huge attention from players looking for something fresh.

Platforms & Release Date

  • Release Date: June 8, 2026
  • PC: Steam, Epic Games Store, GOG
  • Consoles: PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, Nintendo Switch 2
  • Languages: Multiple language support, including Chinese

Final Thought

Solarpunk is more than just another survival game. It is a gentle sky-bound adventure about building, exploring, and living in balance with the world around you. Whether you play solo or with friends, the game invites you to slow down, create something meaningful, and enjoy the journey.
