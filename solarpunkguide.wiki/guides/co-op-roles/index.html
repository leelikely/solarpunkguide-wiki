Solarpunk 4-Player Co-op Guide: Best Roles and Team Tips

Solarpunk supports up to four players in co-op, and having clear team roles can make a huge difference. Here’s a practical way to divide responsibilities and play more efficiently together.

Recommended Roles

  • 🧱 Builder — handles layout, base construction, and decoration
  • ⚙️ Engineer — manages energy and automation
  • 🌾 Farmer — focuses on food, crops, and animal care
  • 🚀 Explorer — travels with the airship and gathers rare materials

Why Role Splitting Helps

A clear role split prevents everyone from doing the same task at once. That means fewer bottlenecks, faster growth, and less confusion.

Best Team Tips

  • Keep one shared storage area for resources
  • Use voice chat if possible
  • Let each player focus on their own specialty
  • Share important blueprints and discoveries quickly

Co-op Strategy Advice

The best co-op runs are the ones where everyone knows their job and still helps the team when needed. Be flexible, communicate clearly, and build together.
