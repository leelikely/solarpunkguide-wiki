Solarpunk Resource Guide: All Basic Materials, Locations, and Uses

Resources are the foundation of progress in Solarpunk. This guide explains where the most important materials come from and what they are used for.

Basic Natural Resources

  • 🌲 Wood — from trees
  • 🪨 Stone — from rock deposits
  • 🏖️ Sand — from sandy terrain
  • 🧱 Clay — from riverbanks and soil areas
  • ⛏️ Iron Ore — from mineral-rich stone deposits

Agricultural Resources

  • 🍓 Berries
  • 🌾 Wheat
  • 🌿 Cotton
  • 🌻 Sunflowers

Animal Resources

  • 🥚 Eggs
  • 🐑 Wool
  • 🥛 Milk
  • 🍄 Truffles

Energy Resources

  • ☀️ Solar power
  • 🌬️ Wind power
  • 💧 Water power

Rare Resources

  • ✨ Gold
  • 💎 Diamonds

Why Resource Knowledge Matters

Knowing where each resource comes from helps you plan faster, build smarter, and avoid wasting time searching for materials you could have prepared for in advance.
