Beginner Guide

If you are completely new to Solarpunk, this page is the best place to begin. It gives you a simple overview of what to focus on first, how to avoid early mistakes, and where to go next when you want more detail.

What New Players Should Focus On

The early game is the easiest time to build good habits. Instead of trying to do everything at once, focus on learning the basic systems, understanding how resources flow, and building in a way that feels stable.

A strong start usually comes from keeping things simple. Learn the purpose of each building, watch how your setup works, and avoid making changes too quickly before you understand the result.

Common Beginner Mistakes

Many new players rush into expansion before their foundation is ready. That often leads to inefficiency, wasted resources, or a setup that becomes hard to manage later.

  • Expanding too quickly
  • Building without a clear purpose
  • Ignoring resource flow
  • Trying to optimize everything too early
